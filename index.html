Government Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-Kisan)

Description: PM-Kisan provides income support to small and marginal farmers by offering direct income support of ₹6,000 per year in three installments.

Eligibility Criteria: All small and marginal farmers owning cultivable land are eligible.

Application Deadline: Continuous support, no specific deadline.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Description: PMFBY is an insurance scheme that provides financial assistance to farmers in case of crop failure due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

Eligibility Criteria: All farmers, including tenant farmers, sharecroppers, and landless agricultural laborers, are eligible to enroll in PMFBY.

Application Deadline: Varies based on crop season and region.

Soil Health Card Scheme(SHC)

Description: The Soil Health Card Scheme provides farmers with soil health cards containing information about soil health and recommendations for improving fertility.

Eligibility Criteria: All farmers are eligible to receive soil health cards.

Application Deadline: Continuous issuance, no specific deadline.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

Description: PMKSY aims to improve water use efficiency in agriculture through watershed development, micro-irrigation, and water-saving technologies.

Eligibility Criteria: All farmers and agricultural stakeholders can participate in PMKSY.

Application Deadline: Varies based on project timelines.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

Description: The KCC scheme provides farmers with access to credit for agricultural and allied activities.

Eligibility Criteria: Farmers who own cultivable land and have a good credit history are eligible for KCC.

Application Deadline: Ongoing, no specific deadline.

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

Description: RKVY provides financial assistance to states for implementing projects related to agriculture and rural development.

Eligibility Criteria: State governments can apply for RKVY funding for approved projects.

Application Deadline: Varies based on project timelines.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Description: PKVY promotes organic farming practices among farmers through financial assistance and certification support.

Eligibility Criteria: Farmers practicing organic farming or willing to adopt organic farming methods are eligible for PKVY.

Application Deadline: Varies based on project timelines.
